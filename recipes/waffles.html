Waffles


Description

As a special treat for my mother, I created this recipe, which includes one of her favorite desserts and one of her favorite breakfast items.

This creation also goes great with French toast, so make this as a special treat to your mom or any mom.

Also a nice way to impress anyone with a special breakfast. I hope you enjoy as much as my mom did.
